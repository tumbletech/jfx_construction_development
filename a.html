About

More About Us

JFX Construction and Development Corporation (JFXCDC) is one of the leading construction and development in the Philippines. It was established in 2023. JFXCDC is made up of professionals with decades-long experiences from various global engineering, manufacturing, and real-estate companies. JFXCDC not only sell homes, it is also committed to building modern, livable, and happy communities.

The design and built of our homes are planned to make it conducive for its residents to Build a Better Future.
